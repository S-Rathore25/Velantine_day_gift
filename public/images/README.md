# How to Add Your Images and Videos

## Step 1: Add Your Media Files

1. Copy your images and videos to the `public/images/` folder
2. Name them as follows:
   - `heart1.jpg` - Your first heart image (I Miss You Bachhhhaaa)
   - `heart2.jpg` - Your second heart image (Love You Baby Doll)
   - `flower.jpg` - Your flower image
   - `video1.mp4` - Your video file (if you have one)

## Step 2: Your Images Are Already Configured!

The gallery is already set up to display your images. Just add the files to `public/images/` and they will automatically appear.
